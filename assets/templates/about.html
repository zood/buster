About Zood

Zood launched in 2019 and is building apps and services for people that let them live a connected life without having to sacrifice their privacy. Most internet companies make money by collecting information about you, then selling that information and/or using it to sell your eyeballs and sanity to the highest bidder.

Zood is different. We don't do any of that. Our mission is simple: Build apps and services that help people live their life and charge a fair price for it. Don't treat our users like eyeballs for staring at ads.

Further, Zood backs up this promise with end-to-end encryption, so you don't even have to trust us to not sell you out. Our first product, Zood Location, is now available and we're hoping to make more privacy preserving/enhancing services in the future.
